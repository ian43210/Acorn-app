# Acorn-app: Boss Battle Scenarios

## Boss: The Flash Sale Phantom
- **Lesson:** Responsible Spending.
- **Attack:** "Limited Time Offer!" (Depletes user's virtual budget).
- **Defense Card:** "Sleep On It" - Blocks 50% of impulsive damage.
- **Counter-Attack Card:** "Price Comparison" - Deals 20 damage to the Phantom.

## Boss: The Scam Shadow
- **Lesson:** Protecting Assets.
- **Attack:** "Exclusive Investment DM" (Threatens virtual savings).
- **Defense Card:** "Check Official Source" - Fully blocks the attack.
- **Counter-Attack Card:** "Report & Block" - Defeats the Boss instantly.

## Reward for Victory
- Water Droplets to grow the Life Tree.
- Sunlight Points to unlock "Rare Tree" cosmetics.
