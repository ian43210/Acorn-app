# Acorn-app User Experience (UX) Map

## 1. Onboarding
- **See:** Cute animation of an acorn falling.
- **Do:** Set a simple goal (e.g., "Save $10 this week").
- **Feel:** "This is easy and cute, not like a bank app."

## 2. Daily Habit
- **See:** The Life Tree on the Home Screen.
- **Do:** Quick 1-minute daily mission (Quiz or Swipe).
- **Reward:** Get "Water" to grow the tree.

## 3. Social & Community
- **See:** A leaderboard of friends or schoolmates.
- **Do:** Send a "Sunlight" gift to a friend to help their tree.
- **Feel:** "I'm not doing this alone; my community is growing too."
