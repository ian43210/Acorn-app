# Acorn-app Visual Asset Library

## Character: Alex the Squirrel
- **Idle:** Tail twitching (Home Screen).
- **Action:** Pouring water from a green can.
- **Success:** Happy jump with acorn confetti.

## Environment: The Life Tree
- **Stage 1 (Sprout):** Tiny green stem in a brown pot.
- **Stage 2 (Sapling):** Small trunk with 3-5 bright leaves.
- **Stage 3 (Growth):** Thicker bark, flowering branches.
- **Stage 4 (Forest):** Background changes to a lush park.

## UI Components
- **Buttons:** Rounded "pill" shape, vibrant green with a slight shadow.
- **Icons:** Water droplets, fire (streak), and gold coins.
- **Cards:** Soft cream background with rounded corners for readability.
